About the HARDWIRES™ Assessment

The HARDWIRES™ framework identifies sixteen cognitive and emotional drivers that influence how we think, feel, and respond to life's situations. By understanding your unique wiring, you can improve decision-making, relationships, and performance.

The 16 HARDWIRES™ Explained

Zi / Zo – Zoom In & Zoom Out

Zi (Zoom In): Focuses on finer details, systems, and precision. Zo (Zoom Out): Prefers big-picture thinking and conceptual clarity.

UP – Unconducive Parenting

Arises from childhood environments lacking guidance, affection, or stimulation. This can lead to difficulty forming healthy relationships or self-confidence in adulthood.

ABU – Abuse

Emotional, verbal, physical, or sexual harm resulting in deep-seated self-worth issues. Healing requires awareness and restoration of one's internal sense of value.

MOM – Matters of the Mind

Overthinking, anxiety, or intrusive mental loops that limit emotional freedom.
